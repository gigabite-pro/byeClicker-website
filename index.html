ByeClicker

Automatically answers iClicker poll questions for you!

Say Bye to iClickers!

ByeClicker streamlines iClicker engagement, managing attendance and participation effortlessly through automation. This Chrome extension utilizes advanced technology to handle iClicker queries automatically, freeing users from manual efforts. Seamless integration with the iClicker system enables users to multitask or take breaks during class as ByeClicker operates in the background.

ByeClicker utilizes the collective input of users, favoring the most frequently chosen responses to ensure a diverse range of answers. This approach adds an element of unpredictability and excitement to user participation in iClicker polls, ultimately enhancing overall class engagement and interaction.

ByeClicker's standout feature is its automatic session joining, enabling prompt participation from the moment the instructor starts. This eliminates the need for manual intervention, allowing users to swiftly immerse themselves in class content. Whether users prefer automatic or manual joining, ByeClicker ensures a seamless and efficient session experience tailored to their preferences.

Features

Automatic Session Joining

ByeClicker automatically joins iClicker sessions, ensuring prompt participation from the moment the instructor starts.

Almost Correct Answers

ByeClicker picks the most chosen answer, ensuring users are almost correct in their response.

Randomized Responses

ByeClicker introduces randomness to responses, offering variability for users seeking diverse participation.

Email Notifications

ByeClicker sends email notifications to users, keeping them informed about their participation and engagement.
